Questions, answered plainly.

Does my audio ever leave my Mac?

No. Transcription and AI cleanup run on-device. The only network calls Murmur makes are update checks, license validation, and model downloads, and each one is written to a log you can inspect. Turn on air-gap mode and even those stop.

Will it run on my Mac?

Murmur needs macOS 14 or later and runs on both Apple Silicon and Intel. The fastest large model and on-device AI cleanup want 16 GB of memory on Apple Silicon. On lighter machines you still get reliable transcription with a smaller model.

Is this a subscription?

No. Pro is a one-time purchase. You keep the version you bought, and you get updates for as long as Murmur is actively developed. There is no account and no recurring charge.

Which foot pedals work?

The VEC Infinity USB pedal is the officially supported model. Several others, including Olympus and generic HID pedals, are reported to work and are fully mappable, though they are not officially verified yet.

How do I get Murmur?

Download it directly from this site. Murmur is notarized by Apple, so it installs and updates without the Mac App Store, and Pro is a one-time purchase through our checkout.
